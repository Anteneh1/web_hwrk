Temperature vs. Latitude

How does latitude affect an area's temperature?


Latitude are distance from the equator. Temperatures drop the further one travels away from the equator, this is due to the curvature of the earth.
In areas closer to the poles, sunlight has a larger area of atmosphere to pass through and the sun is at a lower angle in the sky.









Geography and climate

Latitude and longitude make up the grid system that helps humans identify absolute, or exact, locations on the Earth's surface. There is a relationship between latitude and temperature around the world, as temperatures are typically warmer approaching the Equator and cooler approaching the Poles.
